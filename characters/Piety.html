Piety

Bio

Piety joined the party in Skullport where she had disguised herself as her father, Vorici. The party was looking for a mercenary to join their crew in order to fill the space left by Grick’s sudden disappearance. Before entering the Xanathar’s Lair, she revealed herself to the party, seeing no further profit coming from continuing her ruse.
