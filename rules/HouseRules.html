    Character Creation House Rules D&D 5E

    Character Creation

    Rolling Attributes

    You may choose the standard array or roll 4d6 drop lowest. No one has ever actually taken the array.

    Race and Class Restrictions

    We use classes and races from any published (preferably playtested) source. We have used Unearthed Aracana material as well as third party or homebrew content, but it must be approved by the DM ahead of time. The only limit is keeping things balanced for other players.

    Hit Points

    When rolling hit dice, if the roll is lower than the average you can take the average instead.
